Annie rings the doorbell and waits. Nobody answers. She checks that she's at the right door – yes, this is grandpa Karl's apartment. She rings again. A window on in? one of the adjacent houses opens and she hears Isabelle quietly calling her.
Annie, we're over here.
She walks over.
Which door?
Number 15, I'll let you in.

The others are inside.
Karl says: We needed a little more space. This place has been vacant forever, might as well use it.
Annie slides her shoes off and leans her bag against the wall. Then, they go to the living room where cables and computer parts are all over the floor. Lucas sits in front of the rack between two opened boxes. The rack's side panels had been removed, revealing the rails holding the various devices inside and their power cables. Lucas is pushing a circuit board into a half-assembled device that hangs out the front. Karl goes over to a stool next to the rack, carefully avoiding stepping on things, and sits down.

Annie sits down next to Lucas and watches as he carefully inserts one board after the other. Karl is looking at the manual and tells him to set some tiny switches on the front of some of them.
