Back In A Sec

missing section?

Jack is gone, Annie notices while entering the bus. She looks around.
There's a note on the table: "Will be back at 06:30"
Michelle looks at her watch. It is 06:30.
James chimes in: I guess there's nothing to do but wait. He'll show up soon enough.
Michelle says: I sure hope so.
They place their bags full of found goodies on the floor and sit down around the table.

After a while, they hear a fast car approaching. Looking outside, they see an oldtimer zoom by. Michelle says: That's him. A moment later, the car returns, braking so hard it leaves tire marks on the road. Jack gets out and waves at them. He turns off the engine and walks to the front of the bus. Annie opens the door.
Where have you been?
I went on my own little adventure and may or may not have procured some radio equipment.
Didn't you want to sleep?
Well yeah... And then I had a dream that inspired me to go looking for stuff. He points to the car he arrived in. It's in the trunk.
Michelle facepalms. Annie and James look at both of them in confusion.
Is it one of these radios? Michelle makes a gesture showing a big box.
Yes, it is. Make this clear when Jack picks it up.
